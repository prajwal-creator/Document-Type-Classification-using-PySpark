It is my impression that the most interesting movie critics writing today happen to be women. The names Pauline Kael, Penelope Gilliat, Renata Adler, Susan Sontag and, of course, Judith Crist -- whose reviews have now been collected between the covers of a book -- spring immediately to mind.
