A luncheon and fashion show will be given on Oct. 14 by the women's auxiliary of the Presbyterian Hospital, Columbia-Presbyterian Medical Center, in the Cotillion Room of the Pierre. Mrs. George G. Tenney and Mrs. Harold Behlke are co-chairmen of the committee for the event, the proceeds of which will be used by the auxiliary for its work among the patients.
