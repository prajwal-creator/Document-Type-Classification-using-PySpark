The Social Science Research Council at 230 Park Avenue yesterday announced forty-two awards, totaling $19,290, for the support of research in social science. The winners were picked from a list of 138 applicants. The grants are designed to assist mature scholars in the completion of research projects already well under way and provide for study in economics, political science, sociology, cultural anthropology, social psychology, history, law, statistics and human geography.
