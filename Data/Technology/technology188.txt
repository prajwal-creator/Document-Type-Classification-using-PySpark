BOSTON, June 12. -- Two New England amateur records were broken to-day at the annual championship meeting of the New England A.A.U., held at Technology Field. G.P. Gardner, Jr., of Harvard, won the 220-yard low hurdles event in 0:24 3-5, bettering the mark of 0:25 1-5 made last year by John Mayhew of Brown.
