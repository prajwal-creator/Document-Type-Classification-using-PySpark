BOSTON, March 19.--Otto Haldor Larsen, the Massachusetts Institute of Technology senior who disappeared last Thursday, after attending the party at which Pauline Virginia Clark, a divorcee, committed suicide by taking poison, was found at the point of death today in the Fenway, behind the Museum of Fine Arts.
