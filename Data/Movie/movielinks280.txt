The Consolidated Gas Company of New York, for the year ended Dec. 31, had gross earnings of $10,222,602, an increase of $2,275,964 over 1914. The surplus, after expenses, was $8,502,857, an increase of $1,383,196. A total of $6,737,613, equal to 6 3/4 per cent. on the stock of the company, was paid out in dividends, an increase over the previous year of $748,523.
