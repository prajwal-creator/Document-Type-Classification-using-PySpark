HOBOKEN, N.J., June 24.--Successful completion of a one-year experiment in undergraduate training in scientific research was announced yesterday at the Stevens Institute of Technology by President Harvey N. Davis. Results of the experiment are contained in a report by Professor John B. Hawkes, chairman of the institute's Department of Physics.
