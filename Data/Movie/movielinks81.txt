HOLLYWOOD, Calif., Nov. 23 -- The criticism by Roman Catholic Bishops in the United States about the alleged increase in morally "objectionable" motion pictures was pondered today in official silence by Hollywood studio heads and by Eric Johnston, president of the Motion Picture Association of America.
