Announcement was made yesterday of the formation of Itinerama, a French corporation that plans to exhibit Cinerama films abroad in a complete touring unit that includes a 3,000-seat, inflatable balloon-enclosed theatre. Starting in mid-July, one of three scheduled Itinerama units will tour France and French-speaking countries.
