The ghosts of olden days will be seen in Hoboken streets this afternoon when Alumni Day of Stevens Institute of Technology will be observed and the "old boys" of Stevens will formally take over Castle Stevens, an historic manor of ante-bellum times, which stands on a tall bluff overlooking the Hudson, opposite Twenty-third Street.
