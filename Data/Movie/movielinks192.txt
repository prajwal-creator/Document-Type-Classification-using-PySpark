Movies showing open activities by gamblers on a street corner in Brooklyn were viewed yesterday by Police Commissioner George P. Monaghan and his two top aides. "The pictures were excellent," Mr. Monaghan commented at the end of the forty-five - minute screening. "They gave very powerful evidence of bookmaking."
