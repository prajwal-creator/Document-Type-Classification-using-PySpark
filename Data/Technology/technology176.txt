CHICAGO, May 8. -- Work on a camera designed especially to be used by members of the Lowell Expedition to photograph the canals and polar caps of the planet Mars, was completed yesterday by William Gaertner, Chicago maker of scientific instruments. The apparatus will be shipped to-day to Prof. Percival Lowell of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, who is sending out the expedition.
