NEW HAVEN, Nov. 29. -- The Yale hockey team will play sixteen games this Winter and extra contests in case of tie in the Harvard and Princeton series, according to an announcement tonight. The schedule of home games follows: Dec. 18, Arena A.C.; Jan. 6, Massachusetts Agricultural College; 8, Williams; 11, Columbia; 13, Massachusetts Institute of Technology; 16, Dartmouth; 20, Irich-American A.C.; 23, Cornell; Feb. 8, Dartmouth; 15, St. Nicholas Academy; 13, Princeton; 22, Harvard.
