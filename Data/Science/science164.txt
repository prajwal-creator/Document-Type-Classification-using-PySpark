Two hundred and thirty-eight students will receive degrees at Queens College's semiannual commencement tonight at 8 P. M. in the gymnasium. Dr. John J. Theobald, president of the college, will confer 180 Bachelor of Arts degrees, thirty-four Bachelor of Science degrees and twenty-four Master of Arts degrees.
