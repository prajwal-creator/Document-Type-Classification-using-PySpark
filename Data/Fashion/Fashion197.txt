WHITE PLAINS, Oct. 3 -- The Skidmore College Club of Westchester will hold a benefit breakfast-fashion show on Wednesday at Bonwit Teller, Inc., here. Proceeds of the fete will be added to the club's scholarship fund, which assists Skidmore students from the Westchester area with annual awards.
