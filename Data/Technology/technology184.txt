Because they were outvoted by a majority of one, the Columbia, Harvard, and Cornell Fencing Clubs yesterday resigned from the Intercollegiate Fencing League. The annual meeting was held in the New York Athletic Club and it was the stormiest meeting in the history of the league. Delegates were present from all the clubs in the organization, including, besides the three retiring ones, West Point, Annapolis, University of Pennsylvania, and Yale.
