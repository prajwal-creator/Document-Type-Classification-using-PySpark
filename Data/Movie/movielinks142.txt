Parents used to protest against the harmful effects of books, phonograph records and even the telephone, as they now complain about movies, radio and the comics, Mrs. Sidonie Matsner Gruenberg said yesterday, urging perspective on these new influences invading the home. She spoke at the Child Study Association of America, 221 West Fifty-seventh Street.
