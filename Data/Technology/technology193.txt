Cornell will make its bid for its fifteenth Intercollegiate Association cross-country championship this morning, over the six-mile course of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology near Boston. Prospects indicate that Jack Moakley's big athletes from Ithaca will repeat their 1913 and 1914 triumphs, and in just as easy fashion, for with D.F. Potter, the individual winner last year, Hoffmire, Windnagle, Cornwith, and Tinkham, Coach Moakley has a combination which appears unbeatable.
