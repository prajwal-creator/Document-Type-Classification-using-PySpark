THE eighteen papers in this book, with one exception, are associated with the Star Island Conferences held in recent summers on the Isles of Shoals, off the Maine-New Hampshire coast. Here clergymen and laymen of various religious affiliations have met with scientists of many sorts to discuss the science-religion relationship.
