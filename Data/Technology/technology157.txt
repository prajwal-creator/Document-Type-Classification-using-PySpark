BOSTON, June 28. -- Six New England amateur records were broken, two were equaled, and two more established to-day by the athletes competing in the annual championship track and field meeting of the New England Association of Amateur Athletic Union on Technology Field. Athletes representing the Boston Athletic Association were responsible for seven of ten record performances.
