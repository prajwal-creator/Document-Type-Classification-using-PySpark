BOSTON, March 11 -- The annual scratch games and the New-England indoor championships of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology Athletic Club, held in conjunction with the First Regiment Athletic Association, were held in the South Armory last night. The events were well contested and afforded considerable excitement, but no records were broken.
