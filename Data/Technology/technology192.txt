BROOKLINE, Mass., Sept. 14. -- With a total of 5,442 3/4 points to his credit Ellery H. Clark of the Boston Athletic Association once more won the New England all-around amateur athletic championship at the Institute of Technology field this afternoon. Clark had but three competitors when the meet started.
