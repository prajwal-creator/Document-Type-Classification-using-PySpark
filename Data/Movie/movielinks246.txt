SALZBURG, Aug. 13. -- Taking moving pictures is barred at Salzburg to everybody but Ralph Barton, a New York caricaturist. Arriving here last night with his young daughter, Barton sallied forth this morning with a miniature motion picture camera under his arm in blissful ignorance of Max Reinhardt's prohibition against film making at the festival.
