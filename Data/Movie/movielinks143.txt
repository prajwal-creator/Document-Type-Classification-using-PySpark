BROOKE ADAMS and John Heard will star in Michael Weller's "Split," which will open at the Second Stage on West 72d Street on March 26. MISS Adams is a new movie star--"Days of Heaven," "Invasion of the Body Snatchers," "A Man, a Woman and a Bank" and "Cuba"--and Mr. Heard is a rising young actor who has worked in movies, television and, of course, the theater.
