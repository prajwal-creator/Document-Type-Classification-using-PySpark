WASHINGTON, Jan. 22 The Commerce Department is having "working level" discussions of proposals to exempt, on the ground of hardship, certain kinds of exports from the ban on sending technology to the Soviet Union, Robert B. Amdur, public affairs director for the trade division, said today.
