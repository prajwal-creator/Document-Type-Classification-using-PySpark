RYE, N.Y., Sept. 30 -- St. Vincent's Hospital in Harrison will be the beneficiary of a luncheon and fashion show at the Apawamis Club here Thursday, Nov. 2. This is the annual fete sponsored by the ladies' auxiliary of the hospital.
