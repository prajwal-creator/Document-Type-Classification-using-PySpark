PLATITUDES to the contrary, there is today a business very much like show business, though it flourishes in an arena far removed, at least spiritually, from the Broadway and off-Broadway scene. This is the fashion show business, a field of endeavor born of the garment center and increasingly imbued with all the niceties of the theatre world except, possibly, ticket brokers and backers' auditions.
