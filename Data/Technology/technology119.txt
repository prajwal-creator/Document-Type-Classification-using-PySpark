CAMBRIDGE, Mass., May 10. -- Harvard overwhelmed Massachusetts Institute of Technology in a dual track meet held in the rain at Tech Field today, 151 1/2 to 73 1/2. For probably the first time in a college dual meet five places counted in the scoring.
