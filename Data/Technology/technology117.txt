CAMBRIDGE, Mass., Dec. 12. -- Harvard's opening hockey games are scheduled for next week, the first contest with Massachusetts Institute of Technology on Wednesday and the second with Boston University two nights later. An opportunity to judge somewhat of the strength of this year's Harvard material will be possible, especially since Boston University appears to have in the making a powerful, fast sextet.
