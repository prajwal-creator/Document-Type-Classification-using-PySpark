The freshman Michael Kidd-Gilchrist had 17 points and 11 rebounds for Kentucky (8-0). Reggie Bullock hit a 3-pointer for North Carolina (6-2) to cut the lead to 73-72 with 48 seconds left. After Marquis Teague missed the front end of a one-and-one, Davis blocked Henson’s shot and grabbed the rebound, and the Wildcats ran out the clock. Tyler Zeller and Harrison Barnes scored 14 points apiece for the Tar Heels, who led by as many as 6 in the second half. UCONN 75, ARKANSAS 62 The freshman Ryan Boatright had 23 points and 6 assists for No. 8 Connecticut (7-1) at Hartford in the Big East-SEC challenge. B. J. Young scored 28 points for Arkansas (5-2).
