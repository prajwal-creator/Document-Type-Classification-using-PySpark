WILLIAMS BAY, Wis., May 12 -- Astronomers of the Yerkes Observatory will study the approaching total eclipse of the sun Monday, May 28, from a station at Wadesborough, N.C. The party will be composed of Profs. George E. Hale, Edward E. Barnard, Edwin B. Frost, and G. Willis Ritchey of the Yerkes Observatory, Madison, Wis.; H.M. Goodwill and A.A. Noyes of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and others.
