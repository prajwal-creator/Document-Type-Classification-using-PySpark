ANDOVER, Mass., Feb. 17 -- The Andover basketball team maintained its winning stride, defeating Massachusetts Institute of Technology freshmen today, 38 to 16, after leading at half time 14 to 9. Sweet and Barr led the winners' attack with 13 and 10 points, respectively.
