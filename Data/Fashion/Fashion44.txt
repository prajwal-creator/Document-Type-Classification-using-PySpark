GREENWICH, Conn., Aug. 9 -- The Convent of the Sacred Heart here will benefit from a fashion show and tea, "September Song," to be held at the school on Sept. 27. The event is under the sponsorship of the faculty and alumnae of the convent, and it is hoped it will become an annual one.
