"Science Fiction Theatre," a filmed drama series produced by Ziv Television Programs, Inc., will make its debut over WRCATV on Tuesday, April 12, from 7 to 7:30 P. M. It will replace the "Ella Raines Show" in which Miss Raines plays Janet Dean, registered nurse.
