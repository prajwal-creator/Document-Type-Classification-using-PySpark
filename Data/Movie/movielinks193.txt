HOLLYWOOD and the keepers of its ledgers are engaged in a milliondollar publicity, advertising and exploitation campaign designed to convince skeptics that "Motion Pictures Are Your Greatest Entertainment." Before that slogan was adopted the campaign committee virtually had decided upon another: "Movies Are Your Best Entertainment."
