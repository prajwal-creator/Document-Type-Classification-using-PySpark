Cornell's hill and dale champions won the intercollegiate cross-country team honors yesterday over the Van Cortlandt Park course of six miles before a crowd of probably 20,000 persons, with Harvard second, and Massachusetts Institute of Technology, third. R St. B. Boyd, the Captain of the Crimson team, was the individual winner in 34 minutes 37 seconds.
