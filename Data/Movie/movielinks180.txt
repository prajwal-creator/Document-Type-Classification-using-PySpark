EVERYBODY trying to tell  stupido everybody else what Michelangelo Antonioni's movie "Blow--Up" really  means, please stop scuffling and listen. You're right. Everybody's right. What you think it means, it means. Like beauty and the eye of the beholder, or you get out of life (or school or "Blow-Up" or something) what you put into it, sort of, more or less. Who says? David Hemmings, that's who says.
