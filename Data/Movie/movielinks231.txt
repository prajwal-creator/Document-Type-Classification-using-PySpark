ALBANY, N.Y., Aug. 6. -- Nine Manhattan companies to carry on moving picture enterprises, with capital stocks aggregating $11,821,(MM), were incorporated today. The largest companies are the International Educational League, $10,000,000; Daylight Screen Company, $100,000, and Topical News Company, $100,000.
