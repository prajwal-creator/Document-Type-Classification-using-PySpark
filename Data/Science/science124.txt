A weekly radio series of science fiction dramas entitled "2,000 Plus" will make its premiere over WORMutual tonight from 9 to 9:30 o'clock. "The Hosts Above the Thunder," the opening drama, will feature Luis Van Rooten, Mercer McLeod and Ralph Camargo in the leading roles.
