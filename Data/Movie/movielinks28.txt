SOUND home movies with conventional super 8mm film at a price within the reach of the average family is announced by Bell  Howell of Chicago. This newest advance in home moviemaking is made possible through Filmsound 8, a system that coordinates the use of a new camera, a new projector, and a portable cassette recorder that tapes accompanying sound during the filming and plays back the result during projection.
