" The Woman in Fashion" by a British writer, Doris Langley Moore, is to be published here next month by B. T. Batsford, Ltd. The book is an analysis of style, illustrated by photographs of costumes from Mrs. Langley Moore's extensive private collection. These are modeled by a galaxy of women famous on the British stage and screen, in couture, the arts and letters and the world of fashion.
