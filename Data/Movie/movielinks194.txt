France banned gangster films from theatres today as responsible for a crime wave in the nation. Marx Dormoy, Minister of the Interior, said he would refuse permits to show films that portray "cynical or violent scenes, gangster raids and shootings of 'American' type, or pitched battles between the underworld and the police."
