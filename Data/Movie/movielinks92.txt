IN the ancient phrase of the prize ring, the movies emerge from the vigorous trial to which they have been subjected in this book "somewhat disfigured and a bit groggy, but still going strong." Apparently, a goodly proportion of the contributors were glad of the chance to give them a good, sound thwack.
