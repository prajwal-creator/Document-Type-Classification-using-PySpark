Oxford-Galaxy. $1.95. REFLECTIONS ON BIG SCIENCE. By Alvin M. Weinberg. M.I.T. Press. $I.95. Two admirable books for the "concerned citizen," each focusing on the problems of present-day American science in its social context. Price, Dean of the Kennedy School of Government at Harvard, makes a thoughtful analysis of the main issues of science and government, and the rise of science as an autonomous "estate" in the political system.
