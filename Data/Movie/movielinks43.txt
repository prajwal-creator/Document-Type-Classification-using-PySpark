The American Broadcasting Company network was reported yesterday to be close to a decision to start televising motion-picture feature films from 9 to 11 P.M. each Wednesday. The change, which could occur in January, would give A.B.C. a second night of movies, evening it up with the Columbia Broadcasting System and National Broadcasting Company networks.
