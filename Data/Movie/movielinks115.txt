THE new lease on life that has been taken by the far-from-dead film industry is evident in many recent indications of ambitious and aggressive sprucing - up. Some old furniture is being quietly thrown out, some refurbished, some new pieces bought and a lot of fresh frills and fancies are being fashioned and held against the walls.
