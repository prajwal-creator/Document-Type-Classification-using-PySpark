BOSTON, Mass., Dec. 21. -- Massachusetts Institute of Technology defeated Harvard University, 4 to 1, in the Boston Arena to-night in the opening intercollegiate hockey game of the season. The Institute's victory was a genuine surprise to the 2,000 spectators who watched the teams battle for supremacy.
