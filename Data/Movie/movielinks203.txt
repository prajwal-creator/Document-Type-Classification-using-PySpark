FOR not much short of half a century "Broadway" has been  the label of the theatre in New York. A "Broadway show" is one that comes authentically from the heart of Manhattan. Unless a show reaches "Broadway" it has not the stamp of this metropolis. And still the word holds food--retains its golden glamour. Yet the fact is that Broadway is no longer a street of theatres.
