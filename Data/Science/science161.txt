WASHINGTON, Sept. 14 -- Science and engineering have developed principles, techniques and materials for the mass production of comfortable and hygienic homes, but these efforts of the scientists to solve the housing problem are being largely ignored, it was asserted here today at a symposium on housing at the centenary meeting of the American Association for the Advancement of Science.
