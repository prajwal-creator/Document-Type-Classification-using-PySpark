The opening this evening -- and it is the last one of the week -- is "The Postman Always Rings Twice." James M. Cain, who wrote the novel, dramatized it himself (with a little assistance by Russel Crouse). The company includes Richard Barthelmess -- emerging from the movies for his first starring role in the theatre -- Mary Philips, Joseph Greenwald, Walter Vonnegut, Charles Halton, Dudley Clements and Mary Holsman.
