The biggest electric church sign in the United States is being made for the Union Methodist Episcopal Church, Forty-eighth Street just west of Broadway. It will aim to outshine all the advertisements of plays, movies, cabarets, gums, prohibition and other joys. It will be twenty-five feet high, and will cost $3,000.
