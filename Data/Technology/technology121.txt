PRINCETON, N.J., Jan. 6. -- Princeton's hockey team defeated the Massachusetts Institute of Technology sextet here this afternoon by a score of 9 to 0, in the second game played in the new Hobey Baker Memorial Rink. The Tigers carried their opponents off their feet in the first period, scoring three points.
