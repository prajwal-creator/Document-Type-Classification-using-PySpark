Members of the recently organized Ladies Auxiliary of the Judson Health Center are sponsoring a luncheon and fashion show in aid of that organization which will be held on April 15 in the Caprice Room of the Weylin. There will be a fashion revue of the latest costumes for Spring and Summer wear.
