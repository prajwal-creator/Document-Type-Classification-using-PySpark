A $118,000 gift from the Louis W. and Maud Hill Family Foundation of St. Paul has made possible the establishment of a Center for Philosophy and Science at the University of Minnesota. The center will work for better cooperation between scientists and philosophers to coordinate research being carried on in many areas and promote better communication among scientists in different fields by organizing a common terminology.
