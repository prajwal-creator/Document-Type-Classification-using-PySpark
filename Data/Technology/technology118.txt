BOSTON, Oct. 29.--Harvard yesterday won the first cross-country race ever staged between Harvard and Princeton by the close score of 27 to 28. Harvard suffered a severe defeat at the hands of Technology last week, and was not given an outside chance against the veteran Tiger harriers.
