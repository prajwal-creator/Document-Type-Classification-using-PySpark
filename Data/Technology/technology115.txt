MIDDLETOWN, Conn., Dec. 1. -- The basket ball schedule of Wesleyan University, which has just been announced, shows no dates with Princeton, Yale, and Pennsylvania, which teams were played last year. New Hampshire, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Amherst, and Swarthmore are the new colleges on the list.
