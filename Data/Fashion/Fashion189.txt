The Women's Division of the Legal Aid Society will hold a benefit fashion luncheon Tuesday, Jan. 21, at the New York Hilton to stimulate public interest in the society's Thrift Shop. Guests are urged to bring rummage for sale at the store, which is at 1237 Third Avenue, near 71st Street.
