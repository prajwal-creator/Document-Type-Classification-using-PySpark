The number of stage folk sailing from New York for Europe today approaches almost the proportions of an exodus. The passenger list of the French liner France, for Plymouth and Havre, is predominantly theatrical. Eleven liners will sail today, six of them for European ports, two for Bermuda, one for Cuba and two for South America.
