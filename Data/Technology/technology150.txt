BOSTON, Oct. 28. -- It was announced today that an agreement had been reached on the proposed educational alliance between Harvard University and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and that the plan of uniting the university and institute seemed in a fair way toward success. No formal vote on the matter, however, has yet been taken.
