BOSTON, Aug. 26. -- Three New England records were broken and another was equaled in the New England track and field championship at Technology Field today. The Boston A.A. scored 88 points and retained the perpetual trophy. Harry Barwise of the B.A.A. broke the New England record for the running high jump, clearing 6 feet 1 3/4 inches.
