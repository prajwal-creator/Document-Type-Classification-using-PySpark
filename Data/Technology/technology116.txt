A new method of combining business with pleasure is employed by the Georgia State Press Association, nearly 200 members of which are now holding their annual convention here. After deciding upon New York as a site for their next convention the Georgians chartered the steamer City of Chattanooga of the Ocean Steamship Company and sailed from Savannah last Saturday.
