A TIMELY address on the subject of "The Drama and Its Relation to Radio and the Movies" was delivered by A. de Wolfe Veiller, from Station WHAZ, Troy, N. Y., last Monday night. It is especially interesting, since the New York theatre managers have complained about Hertzian waves enticing people to sit at home to listen-in rather than seek entertainment at the theatres.
