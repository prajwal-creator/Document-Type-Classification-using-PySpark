Three New York theatres whose stages are usually given over to dramatic attractions, were invaded yesterday by motion pictures of the war. In the Cohan and Park Theatres in the afternoon films showing the armies of the Entente Allies and the Central Powers, respectively, were exhibited, and at night in the Fulton Theatre pictures of France at war were shown.
