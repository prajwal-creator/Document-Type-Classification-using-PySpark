CAMBRIDGE, Mass., April 8 Northeastern, a newcomer to the college crew ranks but one deserving respect, swept the opening regatta of the college rowing season on the Charles River today by defeating Boston University and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in freshman, junior parsity and varsity races.
