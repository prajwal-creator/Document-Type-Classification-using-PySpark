Ten American-made movies have been chosen as official entries in West Germany's Seventh International Short Film Festival, to be held at Oberhausen Feb. 6 to 11. The United States entries, selected from more than 100 products made during the last year, will compete for honors with approximately 300 candidates from thirty countries.
