Spending was not disclosed. There was no previous agency handling the account. ¶Scripps Health, San Diego, named DeVito/Verdi West, San Diego, to handle tasks that include creative development and branding. Billings were not disclosed. The assignment had most recently been handled internally. People ¶Robert Harwood-Matthews, president at the TBWA United Kingdom Group, London, part of the TBWA Worldwide division of the Omnicom Group , was named president of the New York office of the TBWA/Chiat/Day unit of TBWA Worldwide. He succeeds Jamie Gallo, who left in June to join the National Basketball Association, New York, as executive vice president for marketing. Assuming the duties of Mr. Harwood-Matthews in London, with the title of chairman and chief creative officer, will be Peter Souter, a longtime executive at the British agency Abbott Mead Vickers BBDO, part of the BBDO Worldwide unit of Omnicom, who had most recently been writing for television. ¶Lindsay Pattison, chief executive of the British operations of Maxus, London, part of the GroupM unit of WPP, will also serve as the agency’s global chief strategy officer, which is a new post.   Advertisement   Continue reading the main story   ¶Mary G. Berner was named president and chief executive at MPA, the Association of Magazine Media, New York, succeeding Nina B. Link, who said in June that she would step down after leading the association since September 1999. Ms. Berner had most recently been president and chief executive at the Reader’s Digest Association and before that worked for TV Guide, Glamour and Fairchild Publications. Ms. Link, whose contract expires at the end of the year, will serve in an advisory capacity at the association from Sept. 17, when Ms. Berner’s appointment takes effect, until then. Financial terms were not disclosed. New elements include Budweiser’s becoming the “presenting sponsor” of Opening Week, the Player of the Month Awards and the wild-card games being added as part of the expansion of the baseball postseason. ¶Blinq Media, New York, which helps marketers with advertising in social media like Facebook, was acquired by the Gannett Company , Alexandria, Va. Financial terms were not disclosed. ¶Mktg Inc., New York, opened an office in London, its first outside the United States. The agency also has offices in Chicago, Cincinnati, Los Angeles and San Francisco. ¶A digital promotional agency, ePrize, Pleasant Ridge, Mich., was acquired by Catterton Partners, a private equity firm. Financial terms were not disclosed. The agency will continue to be led by executives who include Matt Wise, chief executive. ¶ChaseDesign, Skaneateles, N.Y., and its 10Red Design unit were acquired by Momentum Worldwide, New York, part of the McCann Worldgroup unit of the Interpublic Group of Companies . Financial terms were not disclosed. ChaseDesign and 10Red will operate as autonomous units under their current senior managers.
