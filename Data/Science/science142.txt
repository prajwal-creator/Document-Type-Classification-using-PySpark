LIGHTNING -- Contrary to general opinion, lightning strokes are more severe in valleys and near the ground than in the high mountains according to L.M. Robertson of the Public Service Company of Colorado, W.W. Lewis of the General Electric Company's Central Station Engineering Department and C.M. Foust of that company's General Engineering Laboratory.
