For science, 1959 was largely a year of preparation for the launching of new ventures to probe deeper into the great unknowns of nature. The year saw the completion, near completion or beginning of construction of the most powerful machines yet designed for penetrating much farther than ever before into the interior of the atom's nucleus, in which is concentrated more than 99 per cent of all the matter and energy of the universe.
