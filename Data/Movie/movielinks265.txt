Several clergymen in their sermons today will voice approval of the crusade being waged by the League of Decency, the leaders of which are prelates of the Roman Catholic Church, and which is particularly aimed at the "movies." In many churches this will be the last Sunday the pastors will preach until after their Summer vacations.
