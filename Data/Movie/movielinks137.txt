A NEW projection lamp for home movies that is said to produce twenty per cent more light on the screen than any other projection lamp of equal wattage is announced by the Westinghouse Lamp Division. The lamp was developed with the assistance of Bell  Howell Company, who now use it as standard equipment in their projectors.
