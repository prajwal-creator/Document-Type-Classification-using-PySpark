A MAP that appeared in the financial pages of this newspaper last April would have surprised our greatest historian, Francis Parkman. The map showed the route through the Rockies of a pipeline intended to take natural gas to the American Northwest. And the course of that line seemed to shadow the immortal pioneering path that Parkman wrote about in "The Oregon Trail" more than a hundred years ago.
