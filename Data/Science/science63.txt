WASHINGTON, Feb. 23--For 40 of the country's most talented young scientists, Monday is a day long in coming. After months--for most, years--of studying, theorizing, tinkering, measuring, balancing, it's the day they will learn who among them has not just motivation, ingenuity and intelligence, but also promise.
