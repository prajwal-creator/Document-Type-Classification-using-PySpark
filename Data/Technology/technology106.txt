BALTIMORE, Md., Feb. 3. -- A party composed of committees of the Maryland State Senate and the House of Delegates, and representatives of the Johns Hopkins University, left Baltimore on Thursday for a tour of inspection of large scientific schools in the East. At the present time the Legislature is considering a bill which appropriates $1,500,000 for the establishment of a School of Technology at Homewood, the new site of the Johns Hopkins University.
