The Hat Corporation of America is acquiring Fashion Park, Inc., Rochester, N.Y., manufacturer of men's clothing and owner of the Weber  Heilbroner chain of men's wear stores. Bernard L. Salesky, president of Hat Corporation, said yesterday the deal involved several million dollars in cash, with Hat Corporation ready to invest additional funds in future growth of the Fashion Park organization.
