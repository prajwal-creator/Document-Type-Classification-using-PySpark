A revolutionary fuel cell that produces electricity by combining hydrogen and oxygen was demonstrated last week at Cambridge, England, by its inventor, Francis T. Bacon. Sponsors of the device asserted that it may eventually free the world's transportation from its dependence on oil and coal.
