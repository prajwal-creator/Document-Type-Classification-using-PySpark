If you have any doubt about what Americans want from movies, take a look at Variety, the show-biz newspaper that every week publishes a list of the 50 "top-grossing" films as defined by the theatrical receipts earned during  the previous week. The list is not necessarily a report on the most successful films of the year to date. Rather it's a hot-line bulletin on which movies Americans are seeing at any given moment.
