BOSTON, May 21. -- Ray Murray, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, won the two-mile bicycle race for the New England Intercollegiate Athletic Association championship at Charles River Park to-day, in 4:38 3-5; B.L. Wells of Williams was second, thereby clinching the championship for his college; H.B. Brown of Brown was third.
