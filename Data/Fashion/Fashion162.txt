Many parties will be given at the luncheon and fashion show to be held today in the Empire Room of the Waldrof-Astoria under the auspices of the Virginia Day Nursery. Guests have been requested to bring packages of rummage suitable for resale later at Everybody's Thrift Shop at 922 Third Avenue for the benefit of the nursery.
