BAR HARBOR, Me., Sept. 2. -- The gayeties this week included several important events that brought out a full contingent from the cottage visitors. On Wednesday evening at the Casino there was a crowd that overflowed the seating capacity to see "Harper's Formula," a photoplay with an amateur cast made up from the Summer colony and presented for the benefit of the Bar Harbor Hospital.
