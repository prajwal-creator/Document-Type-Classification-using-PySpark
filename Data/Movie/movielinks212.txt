SOUTHAMPTON, L.I., Aug 7.-- James Lawrence Breese gave a dinner this evening at his Hill Street home for Mrs. Henry White, who is passing a fortnight at the Meadow Club. Twelve guests were present. After dinner, Mr. Breese entertained his guests with movies made in the course of his recent trips. The movie screen and a radio loud-speaker had been placed at the foot of the formal garden opposite the porch.
