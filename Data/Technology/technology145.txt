HAVERFORD, Penn., Oct. 7. -- The football eleven of Haverford College opened its gridiron season by playing the strong eleven of Stevens Institute of Technology to a scoreless tie this afternoon on Walton Field. The Main Line supporters had expected an unusually hard battle with the athletes from Hoboken but the crippled condition of the 'Varsity backfield greatly weakened the offensive strength of the team.
