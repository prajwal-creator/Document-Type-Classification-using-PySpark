ATLANTIC CITY, May 12 -- In spite of new scientific developments to combat mental illness, psychiatrists should remember that technology cannot replace the relationship between the doctor and the patient, Dr. Leo H. Bartemeier, president of the American Psychiatric Association, declared here today.
