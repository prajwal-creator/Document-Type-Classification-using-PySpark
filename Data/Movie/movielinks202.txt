FARMINGDALE, L.I., July 25. -- Buell Patrick Abbott, the 24-year-old Hollywood actor who shunned hill-billy roles in the movies to play comedy parts and devote more time to playing golf, today captured the fifteenth annual national public links championship at the Bethpage State Park Golf Club.
