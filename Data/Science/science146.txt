There are 15,000 Science Clubs with 250,000 members who are high school boys and girls. It was a happy thought of Dr. C. C. Little, director of the Roscoe B. Jackson Memorial Laboratory of Bar Harbor, Me., and of Watson Davis, director of Science Service and organizer of the clubs, to enlist these 250,000 eager students in the scientific campaign that has for its object the conquest of cancer.
