A jug of coffee, a jar of jelly beans on his desk and love of science help keep one leading research director on the go 12 hours a day, 11 months a year. On the 12th month, usually August, Dr. Max Tishler, president of Merck Sharp  Dohme Research Laboratories, finds peace on a lake in the Catskill Mountains--without a telephone.
