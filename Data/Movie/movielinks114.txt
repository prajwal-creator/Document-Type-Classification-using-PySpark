COLOR films for 16mm motion pictures with a daylight rating of 160 and tungsten exposure index of 125 were announced here last week by Eastman Kodak Company at the convention of the Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers at the Statler-Hilton Hotel. The films will be available in limited supply starting this week.
