A CRACKER-BARREL book called "The Movies on Trial" (Macmillan, $2.50) has been compiled and edited by William J. Perlman. Mr. Perlman asked studio executives to join the symposium, but after a long run around and many promises the book went to press without their chapters. Most of the nineteen contributors who came in before the dead line want movie reform.
