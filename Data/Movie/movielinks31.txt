THE National Broadcasting Company has visions of establishing a Saturday night cinema -- a series of outstanding motion pictures that would be televised each Saturday from 9 to 11 P.M. Although feature films fill much of the local time on individual stations, such programing has been done only once by a television network on a regular basis.
