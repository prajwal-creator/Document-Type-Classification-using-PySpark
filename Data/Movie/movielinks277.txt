WHITE PLAINS, N.Y., April 11. -- Mrs. Hester Renwick Earle, formerly a resident of Greenacres and of White Plains, has sued for a separation, and her husband, Theodore Langsbury Earle, has sued her for divorce. The domestic difficulties of the couple first became known publicly when counsel appeared before Supreme Court Justice William P. Platt today to argue a motion on behalf of Mrs. Earle for counsel fee and $400 a month alimony pending trial.
