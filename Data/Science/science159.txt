The Swiss Federal Solar Observatory announces that the present sunspot maximum will be of exceptional intensity. The highest number of sunspots hitherto recorded will be surpassed in January, 1957, according to Swiss predictions. Observations are important because of sunspot effects on the ionosphere, the ionized layer in the sky that reflects radio waves.
