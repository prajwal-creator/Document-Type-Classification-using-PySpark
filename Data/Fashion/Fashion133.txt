The Memorial Center for Cancer and Allied Diseases will benefit from a rummage luncheon and fashion show on Feb. 8 in the ballroom of the Ambassador. The annual event is being given by the Trifles and Treasures Thrift Shop of the Society of Memorial Cancer Center.
