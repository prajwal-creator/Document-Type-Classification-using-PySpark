Hausman, an attorney, announced last night that he was negotiating with several large movie producers in an attempt to bring the movie making back East from California. Mr. Hausman said he had been the successful bidder for the Wheeler Shipbuilding Corporation's yard in Whitestone, Queens, at an auction conducted in Brooklyn yesterday at the direction of the Federal Court, which is supervising the Wheeler concern's proceedings in bankruptcy. Mr. Hausman's bid was $405,000.
