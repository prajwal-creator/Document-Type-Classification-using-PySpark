Each guest at a luncheon and fashion show to be held Thursday at the Plaza is to bring a salable item for the Ark, a preChristmas boutique operated by the American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals. The shop is manned by volunteers and its proceeds support the society's Alteration Surgery Fund for adopted animals.
