TROY, N.Y., Nov. 1.--The Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute football team was the fourth victim of the Stevens Institute of Technology eleven when they were defeated here this afternoon by a score of 13 to 0. The game was won in the last half by two touchdowns made by Bloss and Ford on end runs.
