WORCESTER, Mass., May 23 -- The nine colleges of the New-England Intercollegiate Athletic Association admitted the Massachusetts Institute of Technology to membership last Winter, and now they are sorry. The new-comer attended the annual games at Worcester Oval in force this afternoon and won first place with ease, chiefly by reason of its superiority in middle and longdistance running and in bicycling.
