Novels, dramas, stories, cartoons, and the daily paper have been translated into movies, and now the magazine is to be adapted for the screen. It became known yesterday, before the Paramount Corporation was ready to announce it, that that company has in preparation a movie magazine to be released weekly to the theatres throughout the country served by it.
