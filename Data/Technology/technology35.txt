More than 300 persons attended a meeting last night of the Technology Club of New York at 22 East Thirty-eighth Street. The gathering was held to disouss plans of a campaign to raise $1,650,000 among the alumni of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology for new recreational buildings and equipment at the institute.
