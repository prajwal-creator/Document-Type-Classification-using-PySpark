AN Agfa-Gevaert Super-8 color reversal film for home movies -- it is similar to Kodachrome II -is being made available in Europe this month, and is expected to be marketed in the United States by March. The film is Agfacolor CK-17 Super-8; it is balanced for artificial light and has a speed of ASA 40.
