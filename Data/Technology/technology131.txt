PITTSBURGH, Pa., Jan. 13. -- Professor Harry Nicholis Holmes of Oberlin College, an authority on chemical research, and former Chairman of the National Research Council's sub-Committee on the Chemistry of Colloids, described the latest discoveries in colloid chemistry and the applications of that branch of science to modern life in lectures at the Carnegie Institute of Technology last week.
