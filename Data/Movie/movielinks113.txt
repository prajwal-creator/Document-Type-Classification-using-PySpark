The progress of theatre television, motion pictures for television and high-speed photography will be featured in technical reports, forums and symposiums to be presented at the sixty-fifth semi-annual convention of the Society of Motion Picture Engineers to be held today through Friday at the Statler Hotel, it was announced by the society yesterday.
