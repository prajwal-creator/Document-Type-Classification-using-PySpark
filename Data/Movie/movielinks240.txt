The disintegration of family life today can be attributed to the movies, the stage and current output of fiction in their attempt to "bring back the old Greek interpretation of love and make it synonymous with sex appeal," the Rev. Dr. Augustus Steimle said in his sermon yesterday morning at the Lutheran Church of the Advent, Broadway and Ninety-third Street.
