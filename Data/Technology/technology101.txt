The Democratic Legislature of 1935 began as a skyrocket and ended as a gerb. That, if we recall our Fourth of July technology correctly, is the name of the particular kind of firework which rests on the ground and emits a fountain of sparks, just like Senator DUNNIGAN and his colleagues in the closing hours.
